# CS 144 — Labs

Introductory Java coursework

---

## [Lab00](Lab00/) — Pay

Reads hours worked and computes **gross pay** with a standard rate and **time-and-a-half** for hours over 40.

---

## [Lab01](Lab01/) — Integers, paint, and mortgage math

Three small programs:

- **IntFloat** — Reads four integers and reports **product** and **average** as both `int` and `double` (integer vs floating-point behavior).
- **PaintRoom** — Room dimensions in feet → **wall area** and **gallons of paint** (using a fixed coverage per gallon).
- **RealEstate** — Compares **last month vs current** home price and estimates a **monthly mortgage** payment from a simple formula.

---

## [Lab02](Lab02/) — Chat bot

Interactive **chat-style** program using `Scanner`: personalized prompts and replies, with a **Yoda-themed** personality (object–subject–verb style) and logic around a “favorite number.”

---

## [Lab03](Lab03/) — Fan quiz

**Multiple-question quiz** (Pokémon-themed): multiple choice, numeric answer, true/false, and short text — tracks a **running score** and prints results at the end.

---

## [Lab04](Lab04/) — Word play

**Sentinel loop** (`quit` to exit). For each sentence: **palindrome** check (letters only, case-insensitive), **alliteration** detection, and related string processing.

---

## [Lab05](Lab05/) — Space Needle

**ASCII art** of the Seattle Space Needle, drawn with nested loops; **scale** is read from the user so the picture grows or shrinks.

---

## [Lab06](Lab06/) — Mountain paths

Loads **elevation grid** data (`Colorado_840x480.dat`) into a 2D array, finds **min/max** elevation, and uses **`Graphics`** on a custom canvas to **visualize** the terrain (and related path logic per the assignment).

---

## [Lab07](Lab07/) — Image modify

Reads/writes **PPM** images. User picks **input/output** files and an operation: **negate**, **quantize**, **grayscale**, or **horizontal flip**. Sample `.ppm` files are under `Resources/`.

---

## [Lab08](Lab08/) — Abstract art

Uses **`DrawingCanvas`** and **`Graphics`** to draw **randomized abstract compositions** (e.g. layered shapes, opacity). Includes **extra credit** work such as **bullseye** (nested circles) and **flower**-style figures.

---

## [Lab09](Lab09/) — Spelling Bee

**NYT Spelling Bee–style** game: **7-letter puzzle** (validated by `SpellingBeePuzzle` / `SpellingBeePuzzleException`), **English** word list (`EnglishWords.txt`), scoring, and **`SpellingBeeGraphics`** UI with fields and a **Solve** action.
